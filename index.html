Who are we?


Hannah Strakna, Founder and CEO

The Teacher Supply Network is a platform created by former teacher and web designer, Hannah Strakna. As a struggling teacher in Baltimore City with limited resources, she wanted to create something to meet the needs of teachers working in disricts with limited finances, and thus The Teacher Supply Network was born.

Connect with teachers to trade, buy, and sell classroom materials. Members are given free access to listings statewide. Search for items and use the embedded Google Maps feature to locate supplies. Reserve items and pick them up when it's convenient for you!




In addition to buying and selling among each other, teachers may access bulk donations within their district. Our platform gives corporations looking to donate teaching materials for tax credits access to their target market, teachers in need!

Join Us!

Select your desired membership type and start stocking up on supplies today!

Individual Account

This membership type is best suited to individual teachers. Get access to buy and sell supplies within your state. Teachers will also gain access to all donation listings in their district.

School Account

This type of account is best for school Administrators looking to connect with other schools and donors. Schools may donate materials and also get access to all donations within their district.

Donor Account

This type of account is best suited for companies looking to donate to teachers and schools. Create a donor account to start donating surpluss supplies within your district.

Free

Sign up to join your district's network for free, plus gain access to listings statewide (excluding donations)

Easy To Use

Either select a category (eg. paper, markers, desks) or easily search using keywords to find your desired product.

Save Money

Instead of paying full price to a gigantic corporation, get supplies at up to a 100% discount while supporting fellow teachers.
